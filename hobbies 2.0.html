Elisa Bautista's Hobbies!

Hello! I am Elisa Bautista, and I created this website to introduce my hobbies! As someone who dedicates much time to academics, I make it a point to prioritize my pastimes for my own well-being. In this website, you will learn what they are and why!

My Top 3 Hobbies

  • Reading

    • Why do I enjoy reading?

    I’ve always had a passion for reading, and it’s one of my favorite ways to escape into different worlds and perspectives. There’s something magical about immersing myself in a good book; it allows me to explore new ideas and experiences without leaving my space.

  • Arts and Crafts

    • Why do I enjoy Arts and Crafts?

    Arts and crafts also hold a special place in my heart. Crafting allows me to channel my creativity and produce something tangible. There’s a sense of satisfaction that comes from creating something with my own hands, whether it’s painting, knitting, or any other form of craft.

  • True Crime

    • Why do I enjoy True Crime?

    True crime fascinates me because it delves into the complexities of human nature and the darker aspects of society. It’s like a puzzle that I enjoy piecing together, and each story offers a new perspective on real-life events.
